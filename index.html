About Us/History

Welcome to Christ True Gospel Mission, a vibrant community of believers passionate about spreading the true Gospel of Jesus Christ. Our mission is to preach, disseminate, propagate in the Christendom and the world at large the true Gospel of Jesus Christ; its ideals, ethics and sound doctrines based on the revealed knowledge of our Lord Jesus Christ. We believe in the transformative power of God's word and the importance of living a life that reflects His values. Our church is a place where people from all walks of life can come together to worship, grow, and serve.

At Christ True Gospel Mission, we are committed to:
    • Preaching the undiluted word of God
    • Raising disciples who will spread the Gospel
    • Building a community of love and support
    • Empowering individuals to reach their full potential
    • Join us on this journey as we seek to make a difference in our world and bring glory to God.

    History

    The start-up of Christ True Gospel Mission began way back in 1958. It was eventful and challenging for the three fishermen brothers:
    Akpan Luke Ekanem, Udo Luke Ekanem and Ufot Luke Ekanem on the Atlantic shores of Brass in present Bayelsa State. The Lord called them together as he did to Peter and the sons of Zebedee by the Sea of Galilee. They had initial spiritual encounters in the Mount Zion Ministry.
    In Brass, their work location, they had evangelized and the Lord supported their works with signs and wonders. Even though the people would not give them attention but they persisted until persecution began to threaten their survival in there.
    At one time, the three brothers were driven in a canoe deep into the ocean and left there without a paddle or stick to enable them manage themselves to the shore. Their persecutors thought they would never survive back to land. But God in His infinite power brought them back safely to the shore at the bewilderment of their enemies.
    After this incident, they had to leave Brass for their homeland only to meet yet another ferocious persecution from their family and village.
    At one point, they were branded as insane and were bundled to a juju doctor in Ndon Ebre in the present Mkpat Enin Government Area of Akwa Ibom State, Nigeria. It was at Ndon Ebre they met with Etim Abibiak, a student of a Bible School in the area that was organized by the white missionaries of God's Church Mission.

    Mission & Vision

    To preach, disseminate, propagate in the Christendom and the world at large the true Gospel of Jesus Christ; its ideals, ethics and sound doctrines based on the revealed knowledge of our Lord Jesus Christ.
    To promote and encourage fellowship, love, harmony co-existence, communion and brotherliness among members of the Church and Christians all over the world.
    To prepare and equip men and women to fulfill the mandate of the Great Commission and make them ready at all times to resist, confront and destroy the works of darkness in the life of men and women around the world.
